How to use this Inference System?
1.Import Data From DataBase/File, Click "File-Open-From DataBase/File" in menu bar

2.Save Data From DataBase/File, Click "File-Save As...-to DataBase/File" in menu bar

3.Format of File:
in your .txt file, write:
ADDTRUTH number factor
ADDINFER number_1 number_2 ... number_n deduction
...
e.g.
ADDTRUTH 1 IS_ANIMAL
ADDINFER 1 2 3 4
